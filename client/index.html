About the Platform

FoodBridge is a real-time food redistribution system that connects surplus food donors with NGOs and individuals in need using smart geolocation matching.

How It Works

1. Donors Post Food

Restaurants or individuals post immediate or scheduled donations.

2. NGOs Get Matched

Nearby NGOs are notified and can claim donations securely.

3. Beneficiaries Receive Help

Food reaches communities efficiently with real-time tracking.
